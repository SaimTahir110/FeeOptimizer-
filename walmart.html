Walmart Fee Structure

Walmart charges sellers various fees to access its vast customer base and robust logistics infrastructure. These fees include referral fees, fulfillment fees, subscription fees, and payment processing fees. Additionally, sellers may incur additional charges for optional services like advertising or product promotion. The cumulative impact of these fees can significantly impact profit margins, making it essential for sellers to have a clear understanding of the financial implications.

Fee Type Description Amount
Referral Fee Percentage of item price charged on each sale $1.00 or 10%
Fulfillment Fee Charged per unit for handling, packing, and shipping $2.50
Subscription Fee Monthly fee for Professional Selling Plan $39.99
Payment Processing Fee Percentage of the item price plus the referral fee 2.9% + $0.30
Return Processing Fee Charged for processing returns $5.00
Advertising Fee Optional fee for promoting products on Walmart Varies
Long-term Storage Fee Charged for holding inventory in a fulfillment center for an extended period $0.75 per unit per month
