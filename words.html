WordCounter Features

Counting characters, sentences, paragraphs, and pages: In addition to word counting, WordCounter can analyze various other aspects of your text, allowing you to gain deeper insights into your writing.

Analyzing reading time and reading level: By providing estimations of how long it takes to read your text and assessing its readability level, WordCounter helps you tailor your writing to your target audience.

Identifying frequently used words and phrases: WordCounter's ability to highlight commonly used words and phrases enables you to identify repetitive language and improve the clarity and coherence of your writing.
